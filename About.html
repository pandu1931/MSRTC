About MSRTC

The Maharashtra State Road Transport Corporation (MSRTC) is a state-owned bus service operating in Maharashtra. We provide safe, comfortable, and affordable transportation across the state.

Our Mission

To provide reliable and cost-effective public transport services to the people of Maharashtra, ensuring the highest levels of safety and comfort.

Our History

Established in 1948, MSRTC has grown to become one of the largest state-run transport services in India, with a fleet of over 18,000 buses serving millions of passengers daily.
